Details

In this project, We designed and built a fully functional drone using a KK flight controller, Arduino microcontroller, Flysky transmitter and receiver system, BLDC motors, and Electronic Speed Controllers (ESCs).

It's an incredible feeling to see our hard work pay off and to witness the potential of this technology firsthand.

this is just the beginning. There is still much work to be done to refine and improve the system, as well as to explore the many applications of brain-controlled drones.

As the project lead, I am incredibly proud of what my team has accomplished so far and grateful for their hard work and dedication.

I'm proud to say that we've achieved the proper functioning of the drone, which was our first major milestone. I am thankful to Manan Sharma, co-lead of the project, and the other team members (Arjun Thakur, Milind Gupta, Shikhar Koundal, Shraiyansh chauhan, and Varun Jain) for their hard work and dedication towards achieving our goals.
